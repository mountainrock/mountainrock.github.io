Services


Bri8 specialises in producing information systems for small to medium sized businesses. We have a small in-house team of developers backed by a pool of free lancing software developers from across the globe, giving us the expertise to deliver a wide range of bespoke solutions to enable our clients realise their most far reaching business idea online.

From project inception to completion we work closely with our clients to establish their business needs, online requirements and their marketting message. We work to establish a graphical design which supports your branding, and a practical site structure and navigation to welcome new visitors, encourage investment, and support established customers.

Most of our projects are based on open source technologies and built using Java, PHP and MySQL. Where possible, we try to customise existing open source products. This design strategy enables us to deliver cost effective, reliable and easily maintainable systems ideally suited to a company lacking an elaborate IT infrastructure.
The following are some of the information solutions we design :

  • Corporate Websites
  • B2B Management Systems
  • e-Commerce Solutions
  • Online Publishing Solutions
  • Content Management Systems
  • Workflow Management Systems
  • Data Oriented Websites
  • Mobile Apps Development

Search Engine Optimization
All of the online information systems we create for our clients are fully optimised to bring good, well qualified traffic in to the site. SEO requires ongoing work, however, so we offer the option of ongoing SEO campaigns to all of our clients.
