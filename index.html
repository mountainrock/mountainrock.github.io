About

Bri8 consultancy services is IT service company based in India and UK . We specialise in providing IT solutions for small to medium sized businesses; focusing on open source and easily maintainable technologies like Java, PHP and MySQL.


We provide a full range of IT services ranging from website development and logo design to network implementation and bespoke software development.


Our objective is clear: "to provide our clients with simple, reliable, cost-effective and easily maintainable IT solutions to implement their business model".


Our team consists of highly skilled developers with many years of IT experience between them. We are here to help; with advice at first then with cost-effective solutions.


But most importantly, we will keep it simple!
